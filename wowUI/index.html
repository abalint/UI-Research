All original CSS — no Blizzard texture files. The medallion's center sits on the window's top-left corner, so the circle and rectangle intersect and read as one cast piece. Every region is a recessed sub-frame with its own metal border ring, and each widget — counter, tab, map button, scrollbar arrows & thumb, corner knob, close button, action buttons — carries its own bezel. Metal & paper height come from a procedural SVG feDiffuseLighting grain. See RESEARCH.md.
